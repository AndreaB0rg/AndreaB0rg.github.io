Welcome to the World of Krita

Krita is a powerful open-source painting software for digital artists.

Krita is a professional FREE and open source painting program. It is made by artists that want to see affordable art tools for everyone. Krita provides organizations with pre-built brush presets, which allow them to create digital paintings or pixel arts and add 3D texturing in images. - animation in 2d and 3d - graphic designing - texture and matte painters - illustrations and comics - etc.
